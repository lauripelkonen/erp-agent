# ERP Agent Customer Acquisition Plan
## Phase 1: Precision Lemonsoft Targeting (5-10 Companies)

### Target Reality Check
**Total Addressable Market:**
- **5-10 companies** maximum using Lemonsoft + wholesale + sufficient revenue
- **Zero margin for error** - every interaction must be perfect
- **Must close 2-3 deals** to establish proof points for NetSuite expansion

**Critical Success Factor:** Land deals on first attempt - no second chances

### Ultra-Deep Intelligence Gathering (Week 1)

#### 1. Complete Company Profiling
**For Each of the 5-10 Companies:**
- Full financial analysis (annual reports, revenue trends)
- Complete org chart and decision maker mapping
- Current pain points from public sources
- Technology stack and recent investments
- Competitive situation and market pressures
- Personal backgrounds of key stakeholders

**Research Sources:**
- Lemonsoft case studies and customer lists
- Kauppalehti company profiles
- LinkedIn deep-dive on all key personnel
- Industry reports and trade publications
- News mentions and press releases
- Glassdoor reviews for internal insights

#### 2. Deep Company Research
For each target, gather:
- Current ERP challenges (annual reports, LinkedIn posts)
- Recent growth/expansion news
- Technology stack mentions
- Key personnel and decision makers
- Recent hiring in ops/IT (indicates growth)
- Competitor references

#### 3. Proof Points Preparation
**Case Study Elements to Develop:**
- "Saved 20K+ hours yearly" - break down by specific processes
- "2x faster quote turnaround" - specific before/after timelines
- "50% reduction in purchasing hours" - quantify cost savings
- ROI calculations with real numbers

### Outreach Strategy (Weeks 2-4)

#### Channel Mix:
**Primary: Cold Calling (60%)**
- Higher conversion for enterprise sales
- Immediate feedback and objection handling
- Personal connection building

**Secondary: Email (30%)**
- Follow-up to calls
- One-pager delivery
- Nurture sequences

**Tertiary: LinkedIn (10%)**
- Social proof and warm-up
- Decision maker engagement

### Cold Calling Framework

#### Pre-Call Research (5 minutes per prospect)
- Review company's latest news/updates
- Check decision maker's LinkedIn for common ground
- Identify specific pain points from their industry/size

#### Call Structure (3-5 minutes max)
**Opening (30 seconds):**
"Hi [Name], this is Lauri from ERP Agent. I'm calling because we've helped other Lemonsoft wholesalers like [Company] reduce their manual purchase order time by 70%. Do you have 2 minutes for me to explain how this could apply to [Their Company]?"

**Discovery (90 seconds):**
- "How much time does your team spend creating purchase orders monthly?"
- "What's your current process for generating customer quotes?"
- "How many people are involved in your warehouse transfer decisions?"

**Value Proposition (60 seconds):**
"We've built AI agents specifically for Lemonsoft that automate these exact processes. [Specific Company] went from 4 hours to 45 minutes for their weekly purchase orders. I'd like to send you a brief overview showing exactly how this works for wholesalers."

**Close:**
"Can I send you a one-page summary to your email? If it looks relevant, we could schedule 15 minutes next week to show you the actual system working with Lemonsoft data."

#### Call Schedule:
- **Week 2:** 15 calls/day (75 total)
- **Week 3:** 20 calls/day (100 total)  
- **Week 4:** 15 calls/day (75 total)

**Target Metrics:**
- 250 total calls
- 15% connect rate (37 conversations)
- 30% interest rate (11 qualified leads)
- 3-5 demos scheduled

### Email Sequences

#### Sequence 1: Cold Email (No Prior Contact)
**Email 1 - Problem/Solution Fit**
Subject: "Reducing Lemonsoft purchase order time by 70% - [Company Name]"

**Email 2 (3 days later) - Social Proof**
Subject: "How [Similar Company] saved 20K hours with Lemonsoft automation"

**Email 3 (5 days later) - Specific Use Case**
Subject: "Quick question about [Their Company]'s warehouse transfers"

#### Sequence 2: Post-Call Follow-up
**Email 1 - Immediate One-Pager Send**
**Email 2 (2 days) - Demo Booking**
**Email 3 (5 days) - Case Study Deep Dive**

### Demo Strategy

#### 15-Minute Demo Structure:
1. **Problem Validation (3 min):** Confirm their specific pain points
2. **Live Lemonsoft Demo (8 min):** Show actual agent working with their type of data
3. **ROI Calculation (3 min):** Specific savings for their volume
4. **Next Steps (1 min):** Pilot proposal

#### Demo Prerequisites:
- Have their Lemonsoft version/modules
- Prepare demo data similar to their business
- ROI calculator ready
- Pilot proposal template

### Closing Strategy

#### Pilot Proposal Framework:
**"90-Day Success Guarantee"**
- Month 1: Setup and training
- Month 2: Pilot with limited scope (e.g., one product category)
- Month 3: Full deployment
- Success metrics: Minimum 40% time savings or full refund

**Pricing Structure:**
- €2,500/month pilot (3 months)
- €5,000/month after proven ROI
- Custom development included in pilot

### Objection Handling

#### Common Objections & Responses:

**"We're happy with our current process"**
→ "I understand. [Similar Company] said the same thing before they discovered they were losing €50K annually in manual processing time. Would you be open to a 5-minute calculation of what this might cost you?"

**"We don't have budget right now"**
→ "I completely understand budget constraints. That's why we've designed this to pay for itself within 60 days. Would it help if I showed you exactly how the ROI works for companies your size?"

**"We need to think about it"**
→ "Of course, this is an important decision. What specific concerns do you have that I could address right now? That way you'll have complete information to make the best decision."

**"We need IT approval"**
→ "Absolutely, and IT will love this because it doesn't require any system changes - it works alongside Lemonsoft. Can we set up a brief call with your IT person this week?"
